About BL·HOODZ

BL·HOODZ is an original comic series that follows the journey of four unique characters navigating their world with extraordinary abilities and challenges.

Set in a vibrant urban landscape, the story combines action, drama, and supernatural elements to create a compelling narrative that will keep readers engaged from chapter to chapter.

Follow the adventures of our protagonists as they discover their powers, face dangerous adversaries, and learn the true meaning of friendship and loyalty.

About the Creator

Juan Velez is a passionate comic artist and storyteller with a unique vision for urban fantasy narratives.

With a distinctive art style and a knack for creating compelling characters, Juan brings the world of BL·HOODZ to life with vibrant colors and dynamic storytelling.
