About BL·HOODZ

BL·HOODZ is an original comic series that follows the journey of four unique characters navigating their world with extraordinary abilities and challenges.

Set in a vibrant urban landscape, the story combines action, drama, and supernatural elements to create a compelling narrative that will keep readers engaged from chapter to chapter.
